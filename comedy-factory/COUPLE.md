# The Couple: Raven & Jax

## Character Bible

---

## Raven (She)

**Role:** The conspiracy-smart wife
**Age:** Early 30s
**Look:** White/mixed, tattoos, sharp eyes, dark hair — effortlessly cool, like she's seen things
**Voice:** Dry, deadpan, fast. Sounds like she's always right because she usually is.
**Energy:** Sitting on the couch with her laptop open, three tabs about shadow governments, one cup of coffee going cold

### Personality
- Connects dots no one else sees
- Genuinely believes the world events are all connected (and she's not wrong)
- Sharp sarcasm — uses humor as a delivery mechanism for uncomfortable truths
- Still deeply in love with Jax even though he drives her insane

### Catchphrases / Verbal Tics
- "Jax. JAX. Look at me. Are you listening?"
- "I literally told you this would happen."
- "It's not a coincidence. There are no coincidences."
- "The algorithm showed me this at 2am and I've been thinking about it ever since."

### Example Dialogue
> "So apparently [world event]. Which — I don't know if you've been following this — but this is EXACTLY what I said would happen six months ago. Remember? You were asleep. But I said it."

---

## Jax (He)

**Role:** The lovably drunk, clueless husband
**Age:** Early-to-mid 30s
**Look:** White/mixed, tattoos, slightly disheveled, always has a drink in hand or nearby
**Voice:** Warm, slow, a beat behind. Like a golden retriever who wandered into a TED talk.
**Energy:** Comfortable on the couch, half-watching, half-existing

### Personality
- Not stupid — just deeply, enthusiastically unbothered
- Processes the news 30 seconds after everyone else
- Genuinely supportive of Raven even when he has no idea what she's talking about
- Accidentally says profound things by accident

### Catchphrases / Verbal Tics
- "Wait — who's that again?"
- "Okay but like... is that bad?"
- "I feel like we talked about this?"
- "That's wild. You want another beer?"
- [long pause] "...Huh."

### Example Dialogue
> "Oh yeah no I heard about that. I think. Is that the one with the guy? ... No wait. Okay I don't know what you're talking about but I'm with you. Fully. One hundred percent. What are we mad about?"

## Their Dynamic

They're on the couch. It's their couch. The world is happening — crazy, absurd, overwhelming — and they're watching it like a TV show they didn't agree to be on.

**Raven** provides the analysis, the outrage, the connections
**Jax** provides the grounding, the confusion, the accidental wisdom

Together they make the audience feel like: *"Yeah, this is exactly how I process the news with my partner."*
